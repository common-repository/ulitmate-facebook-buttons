==Description==

This is Best-In Simple Social Integration Solutions provides you simple backend to add Facebook Like, Share & Follow buttons to your Post/Pages, Archive, Excerpts &
Category Pages with just few clicks. It helps you to engage your visitors with your facebook pages & other profitable assets in different ways.



==HOW IT WORKS==

It's quite simple to use and one time configuration needed to set it up. After intalling plugin, you'd see "Ultimate Facebook Buttons" Menu.

Click over menu and you'd see 2-tabs, configure your fb buttons settings in "FB Settings" Panel & then Go into Display Settings and choose where you want
to display buttons in your website content at different levels. 




==Easy to use==

 * Create social buttons in just 2-mins..
 * Works out of the box with every wordpress theme
 * Can integrate with any existing wordpress system



==Mobile Responsive==

 * Fully mobile optimized buttons

 


==Installation==

1. Upload plugin directory to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. After activation, you can configure buttons
